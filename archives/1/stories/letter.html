Letter From Montreal

By Morgan Sea

January 22nd, 2016 - Comic by Morgan Sea

Greetings from Montreal,

This is your gal pal Morgan Sea, performance artist/illustrator and trans lesbian cat mom. I've always wanted to write and draw my own comic books, but struggle with my own high expectations. I want to practice and be good at drawing, but i also want things to be perfect right away. I'll think and think and overthink my ideas, until I totally lose interest in them. The whole process is a mess, honestly.

So I thought, this year for Fun-a-Day, I should just start a daily comic journal! Fun-A-Day is an month long event where you choose a project and work on it every day in January, then have a big group show in February! It was started in 2004 by the Art-Clash Collective in Philadelphia and it has since spread to cities across North America. I've been involved in Montreal's for the last 4 years and really dig it. January is pretty depressing honestly, so it's great for keeping motivated.

So yeah, to keep things fun, I'm trying to relax on quality and just document bits of my life in experimental comic formats. January is also a great time to start because I just moved in with my girlfriend Tali, so this will sort of be a story about our first month living together. I had actually moved in during mid-December, but a day after I did, Tali caught a plane to Victoria to visit her family. Shortly after, I traveled to Saskatoon to see my family and Tali flew in on Xmas day. So that's where our story begins, January 1st.
